The Catalogue
50 Supercars. One Object.

A custom Supercars__c object holds the full vehicle catalogue — 50 records across manufacturers including Ferrari, Lamborghini, Mercedes-AMG, and Aston Martin.

  • Linked to manufacturer Account records
  • 23 manufacturer accounts — real brand data

The Configurator
Guided. Screen by Screen.

A Screen Flow walks customers through the full configuration — from contact capture to vehicle selection, option packages, and order summary.

  • Customer info capture with validation
  • Vehicle picker from live Supercar catalogue
  • Auto-creates Opportunity on completion
03
The Pipeline
10 Deals. Full Lifecycle.

A full Sales Cloud opportunity pipeline tracks each configured build from initial enquiry through to Closed Won — with real amounts and close dates.

  • Custom stages: Enquiry → Needs Analysis → Proposal/Price Quote → Closed Won
  • 10 opportunity records across all stages
  • Closed Won triggers automated follow-up task

Pipeline at a Glance.

A custom dashboard surfaces pipeline value by stage, total closed won revenue, and top manufacturers — built to demo business insight on day one.

  • Pipeline by stage (bar chart)
