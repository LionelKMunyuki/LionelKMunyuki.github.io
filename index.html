About

The man behind the work.

Born in Raleigh, NC, and raised across the EMEA
(Zimbabwe, South Africa, Dubai),
moving through the world with my eyes wide open.

That early exposure to different cultures, economies, and ways of doing things planted something early: the understanding that there was more out there, and that the only way to reach it was to build toward it yourself.

That instinct showed up in small ways at first: self-starter projects, websites, quiet experiments in what technology could do.

When the time came to return to the United States in 2021, there was no easing in. I landed at NC State as a Data Analyst and kept moving — through each role, each certification, each early morning that nobody asked for but showed up for anyway.

That journey eventually led to Salesforce. Nine months in Saudi Arabia, an Administrator certification earned, and Tinkerbell — a live certification prep platform — built from the ground up in the middle of it all. What started as curiosity about a platform has grown into a clear conviction: the Salesforce ecosystem is where technology, business, and human systems meet most powerfully.

The projects are already showing what's possible & the best work is still ahead.

Nov 2025 – Present · Dubai, UAE · Remote
Salesforce CRM Consultant
UpForJobs · Contract

Designed and implemented a full Salesforce CRM solution for UpForJobs — a Dubai-based talent technology and recruitment platform operating across the MENA region. Delivered end-to-end: requirements gathering, object architecture, automation, and reporting.

What was built
  • Custom object architecture — Candidates, Job Listings, Employers, Placements — with defined relationships and field-level security
  • Candidate pipeline with 6 stages: Applied → Screened → Interviewed → Offer Sent → Placed → Closed
  • Flow automation — when a candidate reaches Interviewed stage, assigned recruiter receives a task to follow up within 48 hours
  • Employer account management — tracks open roles, active candidates, and placement history per client
  • Executive dashboard — open roles by employer, candidates per pipeline stage, placement rate by month, average time-to-hire
  • Validation rules — enforces required fields at each pipeline stage to maintain data integrity
  • Email alert templates — automated candidate status notifications to employers
Business impact
  • Replaced manual spreadsheet tracking with a structured, automated CRM pipeline
  • Gave leadership real-time visibility into recruiter activity and placement performance
  • Reduced manual follow-up work through Flow-triggered task automation
