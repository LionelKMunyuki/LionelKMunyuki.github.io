About

The story behind the work.

I grew up in Zimbabwe. Built a career in logistics and freight operations at Total Quality Logistics in Raleigh, NC — learning how supply chains break, how deals are won, and how much inefficiency lives inside systems that weren't designed to talk to each other.

Then I made a deliberate move into Salesforce. Not a pivot driven by trend — a calculated step toward building the kind of consulting career that compounds.

I passed the Salesforce Certified Administrator exam in May 2026. Multiple prep sessions. 4am study mornings. A practice set that felt like a mental war. I also built Tinkerbell — a Salesforce certification prep platform — while studying for the cert myself. It's live. It has users. It's proof I don't just learn. I build.

Platform Developer I is next. Then Advanced Admin. Then consulting engagements that put all of it to work at scale.
